Random Acts of Kindness app

The Random Acts of Kindness app offers all volunteer Seattleites to connect with local charities and organizations based on their interest like in education, public safety, culture and others. The users are expected to insert their zip code and hit the submit button, then the app automatically displayed available organizations based on the users area of interest.
